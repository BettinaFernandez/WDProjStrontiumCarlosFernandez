🍙 ~ Caesar Salad ~ 🥗

𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 2 heads of romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 2 cups of croutons
  • Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 4 anchovy fillets
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil,

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Add your croutons and lettuce into a bowl! For the dressing, mash your anchovies and garlic until it becomes pasty/saucy. Mix in your mayo, mustard, Worcestershire, and lemon juice. Whisk in your oil after! Pour this over your croutons and lettuce! Serve and enjoy!
