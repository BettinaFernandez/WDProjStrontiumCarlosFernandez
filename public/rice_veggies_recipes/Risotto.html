🍙 ~ Risotto ~ 🥗

𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 5 cups of broth
  • Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Sautee your onion and garlic in your butter for 5 minutes! Add in your rice and let it toast for another 2 minutes before gradually adding your broth. Stir in your butter and parmesan. Serve and enjoyyy!
