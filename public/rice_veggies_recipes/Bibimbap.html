🍙 ~ Bibimbap ~ 🥗

𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp minced garlic
  • 100 grams beef, any kind of cut
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 100 g shiitake mushrooms
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 2 sheets of seaweed, shredded
  • FOR THE SAUCE
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 2 tbsp gochujang
  • 1 tsp minced garlic

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Marinate your meat in your soy sauce, sugar, sesame oil, and garlic for 30 minutes. Add your cooking oil to your pan and cool it thoroughly. Cook your carrots and mushrooms separately in salt. These take 2-3 minutes to cook each! Put these in a bowl on top of rice! Mix your sauce ingredients together and use it to top your bibimbap bowl! Serve and enjoy!
