𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1 sheet of seaweed
  • Corned tuna
  • 1 tbsp of mayo

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Get half of your cup of rice and pat it down on a platic sheet (or any maleable sheet that will easily cling to your rice). Place a mix of tuna and mayo in the center (optionally, you may add cheese). Add your other half of rice on top of this! Completely encase this in the plastic wrap and shape this into a rounded triangle. Take this out of the plastic sheet and fold the seaweed sheet around it! You may enjoy this with soy sauce! Enjoyyy!!!
