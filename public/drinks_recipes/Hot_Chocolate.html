☕️ ~ Hot Choco ~ 🧋

𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 1 bar of chocolate, chopped as finely as possible
  • 2 cups of milk
  • Cinnamon

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Heat up your milk! Chop up a bar of your preferred chocolate bar! Gradually add in your chocolate and stir until it's fully melted! Optionally, add in cinnamon for some kick! Transfer to a nice mug and enjooyyy!!
