☕️ ~ Vanilla Shake ~ 🧋

𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 2 cups of vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Blend your vanilla ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract in a blender until smooth! Transfer this to a cup! Optionally you could top this with sprinkles, powdered milk, marshmallows, and/or cherries! Serve and enjoyy!
