☕️ ~ Ginger Tea ~ 🧋

𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 1/2 piece of ginger, chopped
  • Hot water
  • Honey
  • Lemons

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Heat up water and pour it over your ginger! Let your ginger steep for 2 or more minutes (depending on how strong you like your tea). You may actually opt to eat the ginger... Pour your tea into a cup and OPTIONALLY, add lemon and honey to taste!
