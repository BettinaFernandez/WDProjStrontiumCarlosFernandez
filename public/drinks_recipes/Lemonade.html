☕️ ~ Lemonade ~ 🧋

𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 1 cup of Water
  • 2 tbsp Honey
  • 3 Lemons

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Squeeze your lemons and make sure to take out the seeds! Dilute your lemon juice in water! Add in honey to taste, but I'd recommend 2 tbsp of honey! Serve and enjoyyy!
