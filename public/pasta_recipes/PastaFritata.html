🍝 ~ Pasta Fritata ~ 🍤

𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 300 g spaghetti
  • 100g panceta
  • 100g Pecorino cheese, grated
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of pepper
  • 3 tbsp virgin olive oil

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Boil your pasta as shown in the instructions and set it aside! Whisk your panceta and pecorino in egg! Add in salt and pepper for a kick! Stir this into the spaghetti. Heat up a pan with your oil and place your pasta in there! Cook each side for 5 minutes! Enjoyyy!!!
