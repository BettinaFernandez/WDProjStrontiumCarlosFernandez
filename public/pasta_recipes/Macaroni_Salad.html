🍝 ~ Macaroni Salad ~ 🍤

𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 1 lb of macaroni
  • 1 1/2 cups of mayonnaise
  • 1 medium red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup minced carrot
  • 1/2 cup minced celery
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 4 tbsp vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp ground pepper
  • 4 tbsp white sugar
  • 4 tsp salt

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Boil your macaroni in your hot water and 2 tsp of salt! Let this boil for 6 minutes (give or take, depending on how soft the macaroni is). Combine your mayonnaise, vinegar, pepper, salt, and sugar! Add in your veggies! After mixing, place in your macaroni and refrigerate for an hour before serving! Enjoyyy!!!
