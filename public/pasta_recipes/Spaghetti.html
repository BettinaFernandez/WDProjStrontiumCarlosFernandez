INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 1 lb of pasta
  • 10 oz panceta
  • 2 1/2 cups Pecorino cheese, grated
  • 5 eggs
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of pepper
  • 1 cup of milk

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Mix your eggs with your cheese and pepper. Boil your pasta and set it aside. Cook your panceta until it browns and/or sweats. Mix your water and sauce into a skillet and heat it for a while longer! Season and serve! Enjoyyy!!!
