🍰 ~ Carrot Cake ~ 🎂

𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup fresh milk
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups grated carrots
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts
  • FROSTING!
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 6 tbsp butter, melted
  • 3 cups confectioners sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Combine you flour, baking soda, ground cinnamon, baking powder, and salt in a mixing bowl and mix them! In a separate bowl, whisk your eggs and add your sugar until it's well blended. Add in your vegetable oil, vanilla extract, and milk! Slowly mix in your dry ingredients (the ingredients in the other bowl) into your wet ingredients. Add in your carrots and walnuts. Place this in a baking pan and bake this at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 40-50 minutes! Whisk your cream cheese, butter, confectioners sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth. Take your cake out of the oven and place the frosting! Serve after chilling for an hour and enjoy!
