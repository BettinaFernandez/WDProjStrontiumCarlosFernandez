𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup flour

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Melt your butter and sugar, adding in your vanilla extract. Whisk in your eggs! Add in your dry ingredients (cocoa powder, flour, baking soda, and optionally salt). Place this in a baking pan and bake it at 175°C for 25 minutes! Enjoyyy!
