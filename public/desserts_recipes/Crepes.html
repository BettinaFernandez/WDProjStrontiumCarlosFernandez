𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup of milk
  • ½ cup of water
  • ¼ teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of all purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons of butter, melted

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Beat in your eggs, water, salt, and milk! Slowly (and gently) stir in your flour and butter. Pour a laddle full of your batter into a pan. I recommend placing some butter/oil on your pan before doing this, especially if your pan sticks. Cook until both sides are light brown!!! You could spruce up your dessert by drizzling some syrup, jam, or some chocolate spread on your crepes! The sweet and comforting taste combined with the light and cake-y layers of this dessert is a need! Enjoyyy!
