INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • ¼ onion, grated
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • ⅛ tsp ginger, grated
  • ¼ tsp gochujang
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp sugar
    • l
  • ½ tsp sesame oil
  • ½ tbsp sesame seeds
  • Beef, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Mix your grated onion, minced garlic, grated ginger, gochujang, soy sauce, sugar, sesame oil, and sesame seeds in a bowl or a jar! This will be your sauce. Oil your grill and heat your meat for 20-30 seconds per side. You may dip this in the sauce! Optionally, you may grill a bunch of other vegetables such as corn, onions, cabbage, etc! Plate, serve, and enjoy!!!
