🥩 ~ Wagyu Steak ~ 🥦

𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 500 grams Wagyu Beef
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, thinly chopped
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 tbsp mirin
    • l
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Season both sides of your Wagyu steak with your salt and pepper and let this rest at room temp. Grate your onions to create some sort of puree. Heat a skillet over medium heat and place your steak in the pan. Once the meat starts to sweat, you may OPTIONALLY add some vegetables! Flip your steak every 1 and 1/2 mins until it reaches the desired internal temperature of 55°C or 130°F. Fry your garlic in the pan you just used. Top the steak with your fried garlic. Add your onion puree to the pan and add in your mirin and soy sauce until it thickens into a sauce. Once you've sliced your beef, top it with your sauce! Plate, serve, and enjoyyy!!!
