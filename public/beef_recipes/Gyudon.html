INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 1 lbs beef, thinly sliced
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 4 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 4 cups of rice
  • FOR THE SAUCE
    • l
  • 1 cup of water
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • 4 tbsp mirin
  • 1/2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp dashi powder
  • 1 tsp chicken bouillon powder

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Heat your oil in a pot, and add your beef slices shortly after. Heat it for 2 minutes, then add your onions and green onions-- heat this for 3 more minutes. Add in your sauce and let this boil for 5 minutes. Plate your food over some warm rice! Enjoyyy!
