𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 800 grams of beef, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1/2 carrot, chopped
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 6 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper, ground

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Blend your soy sauce, brown sugar, mirin, onion, garlic, ginger, and ground pepper or put it in a processor until you get a smooth and creamy sauce. Let your meat marinate in the sauce you made, mixing the flavor in! Pour your sesame oil in, mixing it, then covering the bowl you're mixing it in with cling wrap. Let this marinate in the fridge for 4 hours! After 4 hours (minimum), heat cooking oil in a pan and toss in the marinated meat. Add in your vegetables and sesame seeds. Cook this for 5 minutes until your food is slightly charred. Serve and enjoy!
