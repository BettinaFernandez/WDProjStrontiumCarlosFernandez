🥩 ~ Beef Stew ~ 🥦

𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 2 lbs beef, cubed
  • 3 pc carrots, chopped
  • 1 1/2 cups celery ribs, chopped
  • 3 potatos, cubed
  • 1 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 2 pc bay leaves
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch, dissolved in 1 to 2 tbsp water

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Heat your oil in a pot, and add your beef, onion, garlic, and ground cloves shortly after. Let this cook for 2 mins whilst stirring. Pour in your broth and let this boil for about an hour-- until your beef is tender. Add your celery, carrots, potato, salt, and pepper. Cook this for 10 mins, give or take. Pour in your diluted cornstarch! Stir for a minute. Plate this and enjoy your food!
