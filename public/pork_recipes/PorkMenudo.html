🫕 ~ Pork Menudo ~ 🥩

𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For Two Servings ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 2 lbs pork
  • 1 cup of potatoes, diced
  • 1 carrot, cubed
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 3/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup of water
  • 4 hotdogs, sliced
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 3 bay leaves

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Marinate your pork in soy sauce and lemon for an hour! Heat your oil in a pan and sautee your onions and garlic. Add in your marinated pork and cook this for 3 minutes on each side! Pour in your tomato sauce, water, and bay leaves. Let this simmer for 45 minutes or until the pork is cooked and tender. Add in your hot dogs, carrots, potatoes, and sugar! You may also add extra seasonings, such as salt, pepper, etc! Cook this for 15 more minutes! Enjoy!!!
