🫕 ~ Pork Adobo ~ 🥩

𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 2 lbs pork belly
  • 2 tbsp garlic
  • 5 bay leaves
  • 4 tbsp vinegar
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp of pepper
  • 2 cups of water

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Marinate your pork in soy sauce and garlic for at least an hour! Heat your pot and cook your pork for about 5 minutes! Pour in your marinade and garlic! Add your water, pepper, and bay leaves! Let this simmer for 40 or more minutes! Add your vinegar and let this cook for another 15 minutes! Add other desired seasonings to taste! Enjoy!!!
