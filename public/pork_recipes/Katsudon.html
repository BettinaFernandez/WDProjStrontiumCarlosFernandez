𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 4 pork chops, skinless and boneless
  • 2 tbsp of all purpose flour
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup of panko breading
  • 1 cup of oil
  • ½ tsp of salt
  • ½ tsp of pepper

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Season your pork chops with salt and pepper (or whatever other seasonings you'd like to add-- free will is a thing, buddy)! Dip your now seasoned chicken in your beaten egg, coating the chicken with breadcrumbs after. Fry your chicken until each side is golden brown! Chop your chicken cutlets up into strips, plating for aesthetics! You could opt to drizzle it with some lemon juice for a hint of tanginess, or you could dip it in some sesame seed dressing for a deeper and creamier taste! Whatever you decide to do, enjoyyy!!!
