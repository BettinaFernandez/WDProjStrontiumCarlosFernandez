🫕 ~ Pork Chops ~ 🥩

𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 2 pieces of pork chop
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 lemon
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp rosemary, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tbsp fennel seeds
  • Red pepper flakes
  • 1 cup of water

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Cut a pocket into your pork chops and season the entire thing with salt (including the insides). Mix your garlic, pepper flakes, salt, rosemary, lemon zest, fennel seeds, and rosemary into a bowl. Grind this with a mortar and pestle to create a paste! Add in a tbsp of oil to hold it together! Place this paste inside the pork chops and spread the remains on the outside of the pork chops. Heat your oil in a pan and place cook the pork chop in there for 5 minutes on each side. Transfer your pork chop into a baking dish and bake for another 10 minutes! Enjoy!!!
