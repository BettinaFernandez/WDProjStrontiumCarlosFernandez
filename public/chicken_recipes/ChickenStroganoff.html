on Chicken Stroganoff Recipe for One Serving

🍗 ~ Chicken Stroganoff ~ 🥢

𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 600 g chicken breast
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 300 g mushrooms
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flours
  • 2 cups broth
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 150 g sour cream

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Season your chicken with garlic powder, salt, and pepper on both sides. Heat your pan and your oil. Cook the chicken on that-- 3 minutes each side, pressing it down with a spatula. Set the chicken aside. Now, melt butter on your pan, adding your mushrooms and onions. Make sure to incorporate all bits of the sauce, scraping the sides of the pan. Add flour and stir it for a minute. Add your broth, mustard, and sour cream, stirring it and letting it cook for 3 more minutes! Add your chicken back in and cook for 1 more minute! You may serve this over pasta, noodles, or rice! Enjoyy!
