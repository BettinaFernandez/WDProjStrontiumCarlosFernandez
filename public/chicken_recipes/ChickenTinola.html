on Chicken Tinola Recipe for One Serving

🍗 ~ Chicken Tinola ~ 🥢

𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 2 lbs chicken, chopped
  • 1 cup malunggay leaves
  • 1/8 tbsp of ground black pepper
  • 1 unripe papaya, wedged
  • 6 cups of water
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 4 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 3 pieces of garlic, sliced
  • 2 tbsp patis
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 broth cube (i.e. Knorr)

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Heat your oil and sautee your ginger, garlic, and onions. Add in your black pepper as well! Add your chicken once your onions soften and cook this for 5 minutes! Add in your water and let this boil for 40 minutes. Scoop out the oil floating around in the soup. Add your broth cube and papaya! Let this simmer for 5 minutes! Add in your malunggay and patis, and cook this for 2 more minutes. Plate, serve, and enjoyyy!
