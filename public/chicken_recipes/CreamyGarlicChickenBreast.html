on Creamy Garlic Chicken Breast Recipe for One Serving

🍗 ~ Creamy Garlic Chicken Breasts ~ 🥢

𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • 1 ½ cups mushroom
  • ½ cup Parmesan cheese
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 2 ½ cups milk
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Heat your oil and cook each side of the chicken for 4 and a half minutes. Set your chicken aside afterwards. Melt your butter on the same pan-- sautee your garlic and mushrooms for 2 minutes. Add in your flour little by little, continuously stirring. Pour in your milk and add in your parmesan, letting the sauce smoothen out! Put the chicken back in and let it simmer for another 3 minutes. Plate, serve, and enjoy!!
