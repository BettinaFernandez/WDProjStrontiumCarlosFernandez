on Chicken Afritada Recipe for One Serving

🍗 ~ Chicken Afritada ~ 🥢

𝜗𝜚 ~ ` For One Serving ` ~ 𝜗𝜚

INGREDIENTS 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

  • 1 ½ lbs of Chicken, chopped
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 8 oz of tomato sauce
  • 3 hotdogs, sliced
  • 3 bay leafs
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 red onion, chopped
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil

PROCEDURE 👩🏻‍🍳 *ੈ✩‧

Heat your oil and sautee your garlic and onions until it's softens. Cook your chicken in it-- 30 seconds per side. Once you've finished that part, add your tomato sauce and chicken broth. Add your bay leaves and let it simmer in its pot for 30 minutes. Add your hotdogs, carrots, and potatoes. Let this cook for 5 more minutes! Season with salt and pepper to taste! Enjoy!!!
